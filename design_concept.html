07 / MOTION & INTERACTION
Micro-interaction Playbook
BUTTON PIXEL POP
Hard box-shadow shrinks on click — gives the illusion of pressing a physical key. No ease curves, instant snap back.
→ click →
box-shadow: 4px 4px 0 #1a1f3c → 2px 2px 0
transform: translate(2px, 2px)
transition: 80ms steps(1)
VISUALIZER BAR SWAP
Bars animate height change with steps() easing — choppy, game-like movement, not smooth interpolation. Gives it a sprite-animation feel.
transition: height 200ms steps(4, end)
background: instant color change
ACTIVE LINE HIGHLIGHT
The currently executing line in Monaco gets a cyan left border and faint background — consistent with the sidebar active state. Feels like the cursor of a game.
